Did You Know?

A single pair of rats can produce up to 2,000 offspring in a year — rodent infestations grow rapidly and can cause serious structural damage, contaminate food, and spread disease.

That's why effective rodent control requires identifying entry points, eliminating active infestations with targeted treatments, and sealing access routes to prevent rodents from returning.

ZAPS SOLUTIONS Effective Approach for Rodent Control

Assessment & Identification

Thoroughly inspect your property to locate rodent entry points, nesting areas, runways, and assess the level of infestation.

Targeted Treatment

Apply safe and effective rodenticides, trapping systems, and exclusion methods to eliminate rats and mice at the source.

Entry Point Sealing

Identify and seal gaps, cracks, and entry points to prevent rodents from re-entering your property after treatment.

Prevention & Monitoring

Implement sanitation recommendations and ongoing monitoring to maintain a rodent-free environment long-term.
